Why does Universal Design Matter?

  • What
  • Why

Social media, shoping, news, you name it and it's probably in a digital format now, therfore it is important that every person, no matter their abilities or disabilities is able to easily access that digital content easily. By adhering to universal design principles, websites become more user-friendly for individuals with diverse needs, making a seamless and positive user experience. Accesability features in these designs cater to those with disabilities so that they may experience the content as seamlessly and with minimal issues.

Prioritizing accesability and universal design not only is ethicaly right but it is also legaly required for certain types of websites such as government funded ones and also in other countries.

Creating websites that acessable not only bennefits those that are disabled but also improves the website overall for every user.
